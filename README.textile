h4. Young Enterprise

Many charities, like Young Enterprise, work with young people to develop their employability skills.
There is an increasing need to demonstrated long-term impact, years after the young people have left programmes.

h4. The Challenge

How can we use technology to track young people once they leave our programmes, keep in touch with our alumni, re-engage them, and utilise this incredible network to demonstrate impact?
